About Us

At Bhumi, we believe that education is not just a privilege but a fundamental right for every child. Our mission is to break barriers and create opportunities for underprivileged children to access quality education, laying the foundation for a brighter future. Through our dedicated initiatives since 2018, we strive to make a tangible difference in the lives of children across communities. We follow various approaches like Holistic Education, Empowerment through knowledge and Community engagement to provide children with education that nurtures their intellectual, emotional and social development, empower them to break the cycle of poverty and reach their full potential. Through partnerships with local schools, volunteers, and stakeholders, we create a supportive environment where children can thrive.

Our Programs

  • School Adoption Program: Under this program, we adopt government schools in underserved areas, working closely with school authorities to improve infrastructure, curriculum, and teaching standards.
  • Tutoring and Mentoring: Our dedicated volunteers provide personalized tutoring and mentoring to children, offering academic support and guidance to help them succeed in their studies.
  • Digital Literacy: In today's digital age, access to technology is crucial. Through our digital literacy programs, we provide children with the skills they need to navigate the digital world confidently.

Through our relentless efforts, we have touched the lives of thousands of children, empowering them to dream big and aspire for a better tomorrow. Our success stories stand as a testament to the transformative power of education, inspiring hope and driving positive change in communities.
